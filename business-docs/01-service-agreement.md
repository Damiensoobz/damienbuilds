I'll design and build the website described in the quote attached as **Annexure A** (the "Project"). The quote sets out the pages, features, and agreed price. Anything not listed in Annexure A is outside this agreement and would be quoted separately.

- The total price for the Project is **R[[ AMOUNT ]]**, as broken down in Annexure A.
- Payment is **50% deposit** to confirm the booking and begin work, and the **remaining 50% on completion**, before the site goes live and before the final files and logins are handed over.
- The deposit covers work already done and is **non-refundable** once I've started.
- Payment is by EFT to: [[ BANK NAME / ACCOUNT NUMBER / BRANCH CODE ]]. A secure card-payment link is available on request.

- Invoices are due within [[ 7 ]] days. Work may pause on overdue accounts.

## 3. Timing

- I aim to deliver a standard build within **[[ 2–3 ]] weeks**, but the timeline depends heavily on how quickly you get me your content and feedback.
- If I'm waiting on you for more than [[ 14 ]] days, I may pause the Project and pick it up again when you're ready (which can affect the original timeline).

## 4. What I need from you

- Your content — logo, images, text, and anything else listed in Annexure A — in usable form.
- Timely feedback at the review stages.
- Confirmation that you **own or have permission to use** everything you give me (photos, logos, text). You agree that materials you supply are yours to use, and you cover me against claims that they aren't.

## 5. Revisions

- **Two rounds of revisions** are included after the first design is presented.
- Further rounds, or changes to scope after sign-off, are billed at **R[[ RATE ]]** per round (or quoted if larger).

## 6. Domain, hosting & third-party costs

- Your domain, hosting, and business email are set up in **your name, on your accounts**. You own them.
- Any third-party fees (domain registration, paid hosting, email subscriptions) are paid by you, directly to those providers. I don't mark them up or hold them.
- I'll recommend providers and get everything working, but ongoing renewals are your responsibility unless you're on a Care Plan.

## 7. Ownership & handover

- Once the **final payment clears**, ownership of the final website design and content I produced for you transfers to you.
- I hand over a document with every login, password, and renewal date. No lock-in.
- Third-party components (fonts, libraries, plugins, stock assets) remain under their own licences. Generic, reusable code and techniques I use to build sites remain mine to reuse on other projects.
- **Portfolio:** I may show the finished site as an example of my work, with a link to it, unless you ask me in writing not to.

## 8. Founding-client terms *(include only for founding clients)*

In exchange for the founding-client discount, you agree to give honest feedback on the process and to let me feature your finished site as a case study (screenshots, a link, and a short description). You can still ask me to remove it later if your circumstances change.

## 9. After launch

- I'll fix genuine faults in my own build, free of charge, for **[[ 14 ]] days** after launch.
- Beyond that, changes and fixes are billed separately, or covered by a **Care Plan** if you take one out (see the separate Care Plan terms).
- I'm not responsible for issues caused by you or third parties changing the site, or by third-party services going down.

## 10. Cancellation

- Either of us can end this agreement in writing.
- If you cancel, the deposit is retained and you pay for any work done beyond it up to the cancellation date.
- If I can't complete the work, I'll refund any amount you've paid for work not yet done.

## 11. Liability

- I'll deliver the Project with reasonable skill and care.
- I'm not liable for indirect or consequential losses (such as lost profits or lost business). My total liability under this agreement is limited to the fees you've actually paid me for the Project.
- You're responsible for the lawful and accurate content of your own site once it's handed over (including its own privacy policy and any compliance it needs).
- Nothing here excludes liability that can't lawfully be excluded under the Consumer Protection Act or other South African law.

## 12. Confidentiality

Anything either of us shares that's clearly private stays private. I won't share your business information beyond what's needed to do the work.

## 13. General

- This agreement, plus Annexure A, is the whole agreement between us. Changes must be in writing and agreed by both of us.
- It's governed by the laws of **South Africa**. If a dispute comes up, we'll first try to sort it out directly, then through mediation, before going to court.
