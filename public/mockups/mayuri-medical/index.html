Our Story

Built on Trust,
Consistency &

Mayuri Medical was founded on principles of ethical practice, continuity of care, and deep respect for the patient-doctor relationship — values embodied by our founder, Dr. V.M. Pillay.

Over the years we've grown into a trusted group practice. Today, we continue to serve Phoenix and the broader Durban community with a commitment to integrity, discretion, and patient-focused care — grounded in our original purpose.
