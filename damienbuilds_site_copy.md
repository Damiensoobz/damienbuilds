## PROCESS SECTION

**Section label:** How It Works

**Heading:**
> From idea to live site — here's exactly how it goes.

**Step 01 — Discovery**
> We have a no-jargon chat about your business, your goals, and what you actually need (which is sometimes less than you think). Consider it a vibe check — for your website.

**Step 02 — Content Gathering**
> Send me your logo, photos, and whatever words you've got. No logo? No copy? Genuinely fine — half my job is making something out of "I'll know it when I see it."

**Step 03 — Design & Build**
> I go quiet for a bit and come back with something that looks like you paid more than you did. Modern tech under the hood, fast by default, built for the people you're trying to reach.

**Step 04 — Review & Revisions**
> Two rounds of feedback, included. We go back and forth until it's right. And yes — "can you make the logo bigger" is a fully valid note. It always is.

**Step 05 — Launch**
> Domain, hosting, SSL — I handle the technical side so you never have to learn what an A-record is or why it's "still propagating."

**Step 06 — Handover**
> You get one document with every login, every password, and every renewal date. No gatekeeping, no "call me every time you want to change a word." You own this outright. That's the whole point.
